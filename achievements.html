ISRO Achievements

1. Launch of Mars Orbiter Mission (Mangalyaan)

ISRO successfully launched the Mars Orbiter Mission on November 5, 2013. It made history by becoming the first Asian nation to reach Mars and the fourth space agency in the world to achieve this feat.

2. Chandrayaan-2 Mission

Chandrayaan-2, India's second lunar exploration mission, was launched on July 22, 2019. Although the lander Vikram had a hard landing on the lunar surface, the orbiter continues to operate and collect valuable data.

3. GSLV Mk III Launch Vehicle

The Geosynchronous Satellite Launch Vehicle Mark III (GSLV Mk III) is ISRO's heavy-lift launch vehicle. It has the capability to launch large satellites into geosynchronous orbit and played a crucial role in several missions, including Chandrayaan-2 and the Gaganyaan crewed mission.

4. Cartosat Series Satellites

ISRO's Cartosat series of satellites are Earth observation satellites used for mapping and monitoring purposes. These satellites have been instrumental in providing high-resolution imagery and data for various applications, including agriculture, urban planning, and disaster management.
